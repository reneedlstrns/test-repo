about me

Hi, I’m Renee delos Trinos! I graduated with a degree in Computer Science and am currently working on my Master’s in Information Systems at the University of the Philippines Open University (UPOU).

I’m 29, living in Quezon City with my husband, our toddler, and 10 cats (yes, 10!). Life’s a bit of a juggling act—working full-time, taking care of my family and pets, trying to squeeze in self-care, and finishing my degree.

It’s chaotic, but somehow, I’m making it work!
